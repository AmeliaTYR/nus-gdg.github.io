About Us

NUS GDG was founded in 2004 by students with a passion for making games. Our aim is to get students - regardless of background and skill-level - involved in game development, whether as a hobby or as a step towards a career.

During the semester, we hold sessions starting every Thursday night at 8:00pm. During the First Semester, we organize workshops to get freshmen introduced to the Unity Engine. During the Second Semester, we focus more on developing games as a club activity. Currently, in-person sessions are put on hold due to the Covid situation.

Every year, we also hold the GameCraft! game jam for NUS students between Semesters 1 and 2.

Feel free to join our Discord server, which is where we post all our announcements and where our club members gather. Take note that we will have to verify that you are an NUS student through NUSync. If you would like to join the Discord server but are not an NUS student, feel free to drop us an email.

We also post updates on any events we are holding on our Instagram.

If you would like to reach out to us directly, you may also send an email to admin@nusgdg.org.
